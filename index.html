Meta‑Learning for Coreset Selection: Identifying Optimal Techniques Across Diverse Datasets

Machine Learning Research Project

Meta‑Learning for Coreset Selection is a semester-long machine learning research project conducted in a team of two. In this project, my teammate and I evaluated various coreset selection techniques across 76 diverse datasets to develop a meta‑model that predicts the optimal method for data reduction. It involved extensive problem solving, designing a pipeline to test techniques, systematically collecting metadata, and building the meta‑model, that sharpened my skills in scikit‑learn, data preprocessing, and model evaluation, and remains one of my proudest collaborative achievements.
